Introduction.

We investigate the formation of cytoskeletal networks as the result of sub-cellular chemical and mechanical processes.

The resultant patterns can only be understood the behaviour of a system where the collective is more than the sum of its parts.

Our mathematical and computational descriptions allow us to gain understanding and make inferences over which components of the system are key to drive the development of the cytoskeleton.
